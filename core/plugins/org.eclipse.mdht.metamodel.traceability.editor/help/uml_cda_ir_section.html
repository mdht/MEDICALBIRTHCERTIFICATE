UML CDA Implementation Reference Section Column

Description

This is the concatenation of the Section and Subsection numbers where the UML CDA construct that is identified in the row appears in the generated PDF. The number itself may not appear as a whole string, as only three-level sections X.Y.Z are listed in section headings, and the additional numbers occur in sub-clauses of the section containing each group of contraints or individual constraints.
